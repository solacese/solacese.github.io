💡 Why real-time data matters in CPG ▾
Challenge
Supply chain blind spots
CPG companies move goods through complex chains — raw materials, factories, distributors, retailers — but visibility is fragmented. Demand signals from the shelf don't reach the factory in time to adjust production.
Challenge
iPaaS and ERP bottlenecks
Integration platforms like Boomi and MuleSoft handle transformation well but buckle under high-volume event traffic. Order surges, seasonal peaks, and logistics bursts overwhelm polling-based architectures.
Challenge
Master data inconsistency
Product, vendor, and pricing data lives in SAP but downstream systems — e-commerce, CRM, analytics, distributor portals — are often working with stale copies. Request/reply sync can't keep up.
How real-time helps
Event-driven integration that scales with the business
An event mesh acts as a shock absorber in front of iPaaS platforms and as a distribution backbone for SAP data. Master data publishes once and flows everywhere it's needed. Logistics events stream in real-time for supply chain visibility. Seasonal surges are absorbed without lost orders. New systems and partners subscribe without rewiring existing integrations.
