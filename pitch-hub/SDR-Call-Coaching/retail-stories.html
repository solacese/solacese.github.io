💡 Why real-time data matters in Retail ▾
Challenge
Omnichannel complexity
Customers expect consistent experiences across stores, web, mobile, and marketplaces — but inventory, pricing, and order data sits in disconnected systems with different update cycles.
Challenge
Supply chain fragility
Demand signals arrive too late. Promotions, seasonal spikes, and stockouts cascade through the chain because ERP and warehouse systems run on batch cycles rather than real-time events.
Challenge
Integration sprawl
Retail IT stacks accumulate middleware over time — different tools for POS, e-commerce, loyalty, WMS, and ERP. Each integration is bespoke, brittle, and expensive to change.
How real-time helps
Events as the connective tissue
An event-driven backbone lets every system publish and subscribe to changes as they happen — an order placed, a shelf restocked, a price change applied — without waiting for batch windows or building point-to-point integrations. Inventory accuracy improves, customer experiences synchronise across channels, and new services connect in days instead of months.
