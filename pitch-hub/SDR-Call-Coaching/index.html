“We already use MuleSoft”
They think integration is solved. Position Solace as the layer that makes MuleSoft work better — real-time delivery and guaranteed messaging at volume. Complementary, not competitive.
New v1 — Mar 2026
“We’re not looking at anything right now”
The hardest objection isn’t a competitor — it’s inertia. Plant curiosity, connect to their roadmap, and aim for a meeting now or a deferred callback date.
